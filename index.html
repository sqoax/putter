Why Putter exists

Most betting content is loud and emotional. Putter is quiet infra in the background, built so you can see your edge clearly and stick to your process.

①

Track like an analyst

Every ticket has a home, from pre tournament outrights to live adds. You see performance by player, market, and season, not just vibes after a win or loss.

②

Quantify your edge

Tools like PVI turn “nice hit” into measurable over performance, or under performance, relative to the odds you accepted.

③

Allocate with intent

One unit does not need to mean one bet. Allocation tools help you spread risk across a card without guessing stake sizes each time.

Build roadmap

Putter will roll out in layers. The first version focuses on golf outrights and futures, then expands into other markets and more automation.

phase one
Foundation, tracking, and PVI
Internal tools cleaned up for public use, golf focused PVI analyzer, sharable dashboards, simple import flows from sheets.
phase two
Allocation and edge surfacing
Allocation engine, line comparison helpers, optional Discord integration, and clearer reporting across seasons and sports.
phase three
Community and verified records
Verified track records for sharers, optional public profiles, and ways to see who actually wins over time without hype.
Stay close to the build.

Putter will open quietly, in small waves. If you want first access, share a line or two about how you bet and what you would use this for.
